Consensus Facts

  • On Friday, July 17, 2026, President Donald Trump posted on Truth Social threatening to add the cost of Canadian wildfire smoke to tariffs Canada already pays.
  • Trump wrote that the U.S. is being 'unnecessarily invaded by filthy, polluted, and unhealthy air, the quality of which is dangerous, and totally unacceptable!'
  • Trump accused Canada of not 'properly maintaining their Forests, and Brush therein' and characterized this as 'Willful Negligence.'
  • Trump called the cost of the pollution 'incalculable.'
  • Trump said he planned to call Canadian Prime Minister Mark Carney to discuss the wildfires.
  • Canadian wildfire smoke drifted into U.S. cities, degrading air quality and triggering health warnings and disruptions to outdoor activities.
