Consensus Facts

  • The Trump administration is halting/deferring more than $1 billion in federal Medicaid payments to California and Minnesota, announced Tuesday (reported in full by Axios and CBS News).
  • The specific amounts are $867.5 million for California and $199 million for Minnesota (Axios and CBS News).
  • The action was announced by HHS Secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr., with CMS Administrator Dr. Mehmet Oz also making statements (Axios and CBS News).
  • The California deferral followed a CMS review of certain in-home care claims where spending growth exceeded national trends (Axios and CBS News).
  • The Minnesota deferral followed a CMS review of claims in 14 high-risk/service areas requiring additional documentation (Axios and CBS News).
  • Oz stated: 'CMS is done trying to chase down stolen and misused funds after they've already left the building' (Axios and CBS News).
  • The administration frames the moves as part of a broader crackdown on fraud in federal health programs (Axios and CBS News).
