Consensus Facts

  • Yemen's Iran-aligned Houthi rebels announced on Monday, July 20, 2026, a maritime blockade/embargo against Saudi Arabia, effective immediately.
  • The Houthis framed the move using the phrase 'an eye for an eye' and described it as targeting the 'criminal Saudi enemy.'
  • The Houthis said the blockade was a response to what they called an unjust, oppressive Saudi siege on Yemen.
  • The blockade is understood to target ships carrying Saudi exports transiting the Bab el-Mandeb Strait, the gateway to the Red Sea.
  • The announcement was delivered via a statement from the Houthis' armed forces / military spokesman Yahya Saree (Sarea).
  • The move comes amid an escalating US-Iran conflict and threatens global energy supplies and trade beyond the Gulf.
