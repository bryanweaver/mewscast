Consensus Facts

  • Russia launched a large-scale overnight missile and drone attack on Ukraine on the night of June 1-2, 2026, targeting multiple cities including Kyiv and Dnipro.
  • Ukraine's air force reported Russia fired 656 drones and 73 missiles of various types in the attack.
  • At least six people were killed in Kyiv as a result of the strikes.
  • Multiple people, including children, were killed in Dnipro when a residential apartment building was struck and collapsed.
