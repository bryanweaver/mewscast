Consensus Facts

  • A tropical depression has formed in the Gulf, west of the Florida peninsula, per the National Hurricane Center.
  • The system is expected to become a tropical storm named Bertha, the second named storm of the Atlantic season (Arthur was the first).
  • Both outlets cite the NHC advising interests along the northern Gulf coast — Florida, Alabama, Mississippi, and Louisiana — to monitor the system.
  • The system is expected to bring heavy rain to portions of the northern Gulf coast over the coming days.
